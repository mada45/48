About

48TIMER Festival is a cultural festival on Nørrebro. Together with visitors from all over Copenhagen, we celebrate everything special about our district and pay homage to all the personal, nerdy and quirky we find at Nørrebro for two whole days. The content of the festival is created by local actors consisting of associations, organizations, artists, traders, residents and other enthusiasts. The elements and the many events are geographically distributed throughout Nørrebro, gathered and made visible by a coordinating festival office.
The 48TIMER Festival saw the light of day in 2012 in collaboration with 40 organizers and up to 6,000 visitors. The festival has only grown since then, and in 2019 the program consisted of over 100 unique cultural events with over 10,000 happy festival guests.

In 2020, the festival will take place on the weekend of 15-17. May, and we look forward to introducing you to everything from music, art, talks, performances and dining experiences over two windy days. Common to the events is that they present an aspect of Nørrebro's culture that is not in public view every day. We dare to promise that there is something for everyone!
48TIMER Festival is created by the cultural houses Union, The Foundry, Osramhuset and Nørrebrohallen and the organizers group KulturSkaberne in collaboration with local actors and volunteers. To all our partners, sponsors, actors and volunteers, a huge THANK YOU! You are helping to make Nørrebro's own cultural festival something very special, and it is you who make Nørrebro such a cool neighborhood!
